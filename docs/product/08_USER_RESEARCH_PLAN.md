# 08 — USER RESEARCH PLAN

## Goals
1. Validate the V3 persona definitions against real Indonesian community / brand / company stakeholders.
2. Quantify friction in register / login / community creation / event registration.
3. Surface trust gaps in collaboration and donation flows.

## Method
- **Quantitative**: 5-second test, task-completion rate, SUS.
- **Qualitative**: 30-min moderated interviews (n = 8), 1-week diary study (n = 6).
- **Diary**: members joining 1 community and 1 event end-to-end.

## Target segments
| Segment | Recruit | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Active community admins | 5 | Owner of ≥ 50-member community |
| Casual members | 5 | Joined ≥ 1 community in last 3 months |
| Brand / company growth marketers | 3 | Run community campaigns |
| KomunaID platform team | 2 | Internal perspective |

## Timeline
| Week | Activity |
|---|---|
| 1 | Recruit, screener, consent |
| 2 | Moderated interviews |
| 3 | Diary study launch |
| 4 | Diary study close, synthesis |
| 5 | Insight report and persona refresh |

## Deliverables
- Insight report
- Updated personas in `09_USER_PERSONA.md`
- Updated journey in `10_CUSTOMER_JOURNEY.md`
